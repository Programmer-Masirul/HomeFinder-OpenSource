Real estate managing broker

Being a real estate broker isn’t the same thing as being a real estate agent, even though the two terms are often informally used interchangeably.They guide buyers through properties and assist them in finding real estate that meets their needs. Real estate agents help sellers by listing, marketing and showing their properties.

2

Commercial real estate agent

A commercial agent helps businesses choose and secure locations that are going to boost their bottom line. While sometimes that means leasing great office space, other times it can be finding the perfect location for the next major coffee chain to build a store.

3

Real estate Leasing agent

A leasing agent often acts as a representative of property owners, meeting with potential tenants and providing property tours. The agent can screen applicants, determine qualifications for tenants and process lease documents.

4

Mortgage loan originator

5

Property manager

A property manager oversees a rental property and manages the administrative duties associated with marketing the location, negotiating leases and maintaining the property.

Architect engineer

An Architect purchases properties and oversees residential or commercial development. The developer ensures compliance with zoning ordinances and other regulations, obtains building permits and oversees the labor and construction taking place on-site.
